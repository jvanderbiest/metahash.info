Buying MetaHash Coins (MHC)

Buy on CEX.IO exchange

1. Create an account

Head over to CEX.IO and create a new account. During that process, they will ask you for identification details. This is a normal procedure and it's in place to avoid fraud and other potentially illegal activities. So, nothing to worry about as it's a standard procedure that would take you a couple of minutes extra.

2. Fund your account

Login to CEX.IO and look out for a "Deposit" button. We want to transfer some FIAT currency (USD, EUR, GBP, RUB) to our account. Depending on your location, you should see similar options to deposit. Make sure you deposit the FIAT that is supported as trading pairs for MHC. For instance USD/MHC is a trading pair, so you are able to buy MHC with US Dollars.

3. Buy MHC

Select the trading pair that you are interested in. If you check under the chart, there is an option to buy MHC. There are 3 different ways to buy. Limit and Market we will cover, margin trading is too advanced and out of scope.

3.1. Limit Buy

Use this method if you want to buy MHC for a specific price. Once the price is reached and someone is willing to sell at that price, your order will execute.

3.2. Market Buy

Use this method if you want to buy MHC for the current market price. Your order will be fulfilled instantly. Market orders are used when certainty of execution is a priority over the price of execution.
