Make money while you sleep

Forging and delegation

If you have your MetaHash coins in a MetaWallet, you can generate a passive income. You need to use the official MetaGate application. Try your luck with the forging lottery or use coin delegation to receive a daily reward from the MHC network.
Two types of passive income

Forging

To take part in forging, you only need 100 coins for passive and 1000 coins to take part in active forging. Active forging requires you to keep the MetaGate application open for 4-hours to create a lottery ticket. Read through the official documentation to learn more about lottery forging.

Coin delegation

With delegation, you would freeze any amount of coins (minimum of 512 coins) to delegate to a server node. Don't worry, you still own the coins and you are guaranteed to get rewarded for doing this. You are rewarded by the MHC network and by the server node, you could expect an annual yield of 30%. Head over to the official documentation to start delegation.
